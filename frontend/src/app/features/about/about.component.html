What is Mastermind?

Mastermind is a logic and deduction game. The goal is to discover a secret combination of 4 colors generated by the system. With each attempt, you receive clues about how close you are to the correct answer.

How to Play

01

Choose the difficulty

Select between Easy, Medium, or Hard before starting.

02

Build your guess

Click the colors in the panel to fill the 4 slots in the active row.

03

Submit and analyze

Click "Submit Guess" and use the feedback to refine your next attempt.

04

Crack it within 10 attempts

The fewer attempts you need to solve the combination, the higher your score.

Understanding Feedback

Black - Exact match

The color is correct and in the right position.

White - Correct color, wrong position

The color exists in the combination, but in a different position.

Empty - Color not present

That color is not part of the secret combination.

Difficulties

Easy 6 colors available
Medium 8 colors available
Hard 10 colors available

Scoring

Your score is calculated based on how many attempts remain when you crack the combination:

remaining attempts x 100
Solved on the 1st attempt 900 pts
Solved on the 5th attempt 500 pts
Solved on the 10th attempt 0 pts
